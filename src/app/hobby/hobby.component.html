Bill Gates Hobby

Reading

Bill Gates is an avid reader, averaging one book a week. On vacation, he carries half a library with him. His main reason for reading: “keep learning”. Whether you’re reading a voluminous biography, a scholarly work, a truthful novel, or a blog like this one: anything you can learn from, no matter how small or how big, makes you a richer person. In Gates’ case, you can’t take that more literally.
